Competitive Running

Competitive endurance running comes in a great amount of variety, and can range from a few laps on a track field, to running a whole marathon. In each race, the athletes are challenged to see who can run the fastest for the longest amount of time. The athletes need to find the right balance between speed and endurance to come out on top. Running too fast will cause a greater depletion of energy while running too slow will cause the athlete to fall behind. Competing in a running race is a difficult challenge and can take many years of training to even compete in some of the longest runs.
