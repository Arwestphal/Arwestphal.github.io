Endurance Running

Endurance running is an athletic activity in which someone runs for an extensive amount of time, intending to challenge the body with continuous movement. Running is a very difficult activity as it is very depriving of energy and requires mental discipline. Running is a crucial part of athleticism because it increases the body’s ability to maintain its energy for longer periods of time and to recover faster from energy deprivation. Besides being an athletic sport, running is still a great activity for anyone trying to improve their physical health, and potentially improve their qualtiy of life. There are many different reasons why running can be beneficial to everyone, especially to those who are most willing.

Why Start?

"Endurance running is as physical as it is mental. A workout of the muscles and the mind that tests our limits over hours and miles. And like most challenges, long-distance running can come with great rewards, no matter your pace or personal best. Done right — with adequate nutrient intake, attentive recovery and any necessary medical clearance — endurance running has the potential to change your mind and body from the cell level up, making your heart function better, transforming your muscles and improving your memory." - abbott.com
